Demigod Daycare (Team)

May 2022 - August 2022

Project Description

Demigod Daycare is a strategy game where you must enroll multiple different demigods, the children of the Greek. Then choose 3-5 of your daycare's babies to compete with another daycare's team full of demigod babies. Choose the order of team and strategic ability combos in order to beat the other team and gain drachma so you can enroll more student to your daycare.

Tools Used

  • Engine: Unity
  • Programming Language: C#
  • IDE: Visual Studio Code
  • Source Control: Plastic Scm

What I learned

I was the system designer and a gameplay programmer. I programmed and designed the ability system, combat logic, and made sure of that the game was balance so there wasn't a steep learning curve for players when playing the game.
