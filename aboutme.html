About Me

Hello, World! My name is Manyeuris Soriano, I am a first-generation Afro-Latino. I was born and raised in Boston, Massachusetts where different extracurriculars helped foster my love for technology, math, and science. As a junior engineer, I am fueled by a passion for creating software and games that can help connect people and a strong need to learn, understand, and mold the world around me. I would describe myself as determined, friendly, and goal-oriented.
